Before you write

Frequently asked questions

How old does my child need to be to join?
The Navy League Cadet Corps (NLCC) is for ages 10–12, and the Naval Sea Cadet Corps (NSCC) is for ages 13–17. If you're unsure which fits, just mention your cadet's age in your message and we'll guide you.
When and where do you meet?
The division holds monthly weekend drills at Naval & Military Park on the Buffalo waterfront (1 Naval Marina Park S, Buffalo, NY 14202). Contact us for the current drill schedule and reporting details.
What does it cost to participate?
Cadets pay annual dues, and there are costs for uniforms (sea bags) and meals at drills. You can review current pricing and pay online on our Store page.
How do I officially enroll?
Enrollment is handled through the national program. Start at seacadets.org/join, then reach out to the unit so we can welcome your family and walk you through the first drill.
Do I need military experience to volunteer?
No. Adult volunteers come from all backgrounds. If you'd like to support the division as an instructor, officer, or helper, mention it in your message — we'd love to hear from you.

Ready to take the next step?

Your first step is simple.

Begin enrollment through the national program, then connect with the unit so we can help your family every step of the way.
