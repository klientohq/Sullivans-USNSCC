Buffalo Sea Cadets

Challenge, purpose, and belonging for future leaders.

The Sullivans Division is part of the U.S. Naval Sea Cadet Corps, a federally chartered youth development program for young people interested in leadership, service, maritime skills, and the values of the sea services.

Cadets train in a disciplined team environment, participate in community events, learn military customs, and build confidence through real responsibility. Families can use this site to learn what the program is, how to make contact, and where to find the resources they need.

What cadets gain
  • Leadership and accountability
  • Drill, customs, and naval tradition
  • Service to the Buffalo community
  • Training, readiness, and teamwork

Two programs, one division

Find the right fit for your cadet's age.

Ages 10–12

Navy League Cadet Corps

NLCC

The entry-level program for younger cadets. NLCC introduces naval customs, teamwork, and discipline in an age-appropriate environment. The foundation for advancing to Sea Cadets.

  • Introduction to naval customs
  • Basic seamanship skills
  • Drill and uniform standards
  • Community service opportunities
Ask About NLCC
Ages 13–17

Sea Cadets

NSCC

The full Sea Cadet program with advanced training, leadership billets, summer training opportunities, and a direct pathway to military advancement and college readiness.

  • Advanced naval and maritime training
  • Leadership billets and chain of command
  • Summer training programs nationwide
  • College and military career pathways
