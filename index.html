Buffalo Sea Cadets

Challenge, purpose, and belonging for future leaders.

The Sullivans Division is part of the U.S. Naval Sea Cadet Corps, a federally chartered youth development program for young people interested in leadership, service, maritime skills, and the values of the sea services.

Cadets train in a disciplined team environment, participate in community events, learn military customs, and build confidence through real responsibility. Families can use this site to learn what the program is, how to make contact, and where to find the resources they need.

What cadets gain
  • Leadership and accountability
  • Drill, customs, and naval tradition
  • Service to the Buffalo community
  • Training, readiness, and teamwork
